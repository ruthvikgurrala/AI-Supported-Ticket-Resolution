# Owntrail New Policy Update (2025)

## 1. Mobile App Features
We are excited to announce the launch of the Owntrail Mobile App!
- **Download**: Available on iOS and Android stores.
- **Login**: Use your existing web credentials.
- **Features**: Track orders, chat with support, and manage subscriptions on the go.

## 2. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
Security is our priority. You can now enable 2FA in your account settings.
- Go to **Settings > Security > Enable 2FA**.
- We support Google Authenticator and SMS codes.
- If you lose your device, contact support with your backup codes.

## 3. Owntrail Rewards Program
Earn points for every purchase!
- **Earn**: 1 point for every $1 spent.
- **Redeem**: 100 points = $5 discount.
- **Tiers**:
    - **Silver**: 0-500 points
    - **Gold**: 500+ points (Free shipping on all orders)

## 4. Extended Return Policy
We have extended our return window from 30 days to **60 days** for all items purchased after Nov 1st, 2025.
- Items must be unworn and in original packaging.
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
